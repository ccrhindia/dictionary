About Us



Homoeopathy ShabdKosh is an innovative initiative aimed at providing authentic and simple Hindi meanings of English terminologies used in Homoeopathy. The platform is especially designed for professionals and students who wish to enhance the use of Hindi in their academic, clinical, and official work. This initiative represents a meaningful integration of language and medical science, focusing on making knowledge more accessible and inclusive. The portal not only provides meanings but also offers contextual explanations and usage examples, enabling users to gain a deeper understanding of the subject.



Vision


To establish Hindi as a strong, standardized, and widely accepted language of knowledge in the field of Homoeopathy, ensuring its effective use in education, research, and clinical practice.



Mission


  1. To develop a standardized Hindi terminology database for Homoeopathy.
  2. To disseminate knowledge through a simple, user-friendly digital platform.
  3. To provide a reliable reference source for practitioners, researchers, and students.
  4. To promote the use of Hindi in official and academic work in alignment with Rajbhasha policy.
  5. To continuously expand and update the terminology with expert contributions.
